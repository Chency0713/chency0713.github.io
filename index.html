welcome to Haukai restaurant

Haukai Restaurant is based in the Bay of Islands and specialises in Māori kai. The dinner menu features Māori and Pākeha kai options and drinks. We welcome customers to experience Te Ao Māori with the use of karakia for blessing food and other Māori culture elements in our restaurant.
